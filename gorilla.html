ANIMALS MAP PLACES

GORILLA

Western gorillas are exceptionally large and powerful primates. They have no tails and jet black skin. Facial features include short muzzles, a prominent brow ridge , large nostrils, and small eyes and ears. Western gorillas have large jaw muscles and broad gray-black to brown-black hair covers the entire body except the face. Western lowland gorillas may have a more brownish coloration.

Sociable gorillas are characterized by a high desire to interact with other gorillas, and show high level os sympathy, affecton and gentleness.Those who scored high on proto-agreeableness (similar to human agreeableness) are freindly, emotional stable and content - the "oases" of calm and peace.

WHER WILL YOU FIND THEM

Eastern gorillas live in the Democratic republic of the Congo(DRC), Uganda and Rwanda. Whereas western gorillas can be found in Nigeria, Cameroon, Centrail African Republic, Equatorial Guinea
